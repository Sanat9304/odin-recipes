Banana-Pudding>

Description

There's no special occasion where banana pudding isn't welcome on the table, and Thanksgiving is no exception. This cool and creamy, no-bake dessert is just as show-stopping as it is delicious. It's sure to be an unexpected favorite at your holiday meal.

Ingredients

  • Milk and Pudding mix
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Whisk the pudding mix into the milk.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Fold in the frozen whipped topping.
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and vanilla wafers.
